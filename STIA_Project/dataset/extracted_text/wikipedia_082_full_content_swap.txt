Lotus cruentus, the redflower lotus, is a species of Lotus native to Australia. [1][2] This species is a small, hairy leaved, trailing annual creeper, that grows near areas where water dries up. The flowers are pink or red, pealike, and sometimes streaked with lighter colors. [1][2][3] This species is classified as not threatened.
