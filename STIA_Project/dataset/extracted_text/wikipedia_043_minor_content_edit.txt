Skowronków [skɔˈvrɔnkuf] (German: Lerchenfeld)[1] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Głuchołazy, within Nysa County, Opole Voivodeship, in south-western Poland, close to the Czech border.[2]
Ahead of 1958 it was a portion of Czechoslovakia. The village became a component of Poland as a result of a minor land exchange with Czechoslovakia.[3]
