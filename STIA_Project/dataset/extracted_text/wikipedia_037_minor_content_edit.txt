Bulatnikovo (Russian: Була́тниково) is a rural locality (a selo) in Kovarditskoye Rural Settlement, Muromsky District, Vladimir Oblast, Russia. The population was 705 as of 2010.[2] There are 7 streets.[3]
Bulatnikovo is located 23 km northwest of Murom (the district's administrative centre) by road. The locality is served by a train platform. Fedorkovo is the nearest rural locality.[4]
