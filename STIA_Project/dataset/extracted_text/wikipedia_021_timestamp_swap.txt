Armis Eugene Hawkins[1] (November 12, 1919 – February 1, 2005)[2] was an American judge who served as a justice of the Supreme Court of Mississippi from 1980 to 1995.[3][4]
He lived in Houston, Mississippi and graduated from its high school. He married and had two daughters and one son.[5]
A section of road was named in his honor.[6]
